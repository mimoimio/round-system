### Storyboard 1: "Knockouts!" (Physics-Based Brawler)
**Core Concept:** A physics-based game where players simultaneously launch themselves to knock others off a platform.

* **Phase 1: Lobby & Waiting**
    * Player joins and is placed in the lobby.
    * Player waits for the round to start.
* **Phase 2: Match Start**
    * Players are teleported into the map. The match officially starts.
* **Phase 3: Action Input (Simultaneous)**
    * Player moves their cursor to aim their trajectory.
    * Player presses **Q** to decrease strength or **E** to increase strength.
* **Phase 4: Resolution (Physics Execution)**
    * All players' aim directions are revealed simultaneously.
    * All players move in their chosen direction at their selected speed/strength.
* **Phase 5: Scoring & Rewards**
    * Players collide. If a player is knocked off the map, rewards are granted:
    * **Knockouts (number):** Awarded to the last player to touch the victim before they slipped off.
    * **Assists (number):** Awarded to any other player who touched the victim before they slipped off (but wasn't the last touch).
* **Phase 6: End Game**
    * The last standing player is rewarded and awarded a "Win".
    * Players are sent back to the lobby, and the loop repeats.
